# Privacy Policy — SpectraScope Audio Frequency Analyzer

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

SpectraScope ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that
 analyzes and visualizes audio frequency in real time. We are committed
 to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

SpectraScope does NOT collect any data.

Specifically, the Extension does NOT:
 - Record, store, or transmit any audio data
 - Collect personal information of any kind
 - Use cookies or tracking technologies
 - Send any data to external servers
 - Share any information with third parties

## How Audio Processing Works

Audio is captured from your active browser tab **only when you
 explicitly press the "Start Capture" button**. All audio processing
 is performed entirely within your local browser using the
 Web Audio API. No audio or frequency data ever leaves your device.

When you press "Stop" or close the popup, all audio capture
 stops immediately and no data is retained.

## Permissions

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

- tabCapture — Required to access audio from the active tab for real-time visualization only
 - activeTab — Required to identify the current tab
 - scripting — Required for extension functionality

These permissions are used solely for local audio visualization
 and never for data collection.
